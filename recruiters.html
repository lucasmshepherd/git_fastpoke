Recruiters, Fastpoke is a perfect way to market yourself.

Track your exposure

See exactly how many views your profile is receiving with detailed analytics behind the views. You can see the industry, functional, and geographical breakdown of your viewers as well as the breakdown between the number of employers and candidates viewing you!

Receive written referrals

Market yourself through written referrals from clients and candidates you acquire on or off of the website. Showcase your past placements and business to generate interest. You may request referrals by sending a custom link. Easy and seamless.

Receive requisitions

Hiring authorities can share an opening with you personally by sending one to your inbox. A requisition may only be sent to 3 recruiters maximum for contingency placements so you won't have to worry about cutthroat competition. Relationships and collaboration is what we're all about.

Schedule calls seamlessly

By syncing your personal calendar with the platform, you can create time slots for clients and candidates to schedule calls with you. Allow your referrals to do the talking and keep your calendar open for introductions.
