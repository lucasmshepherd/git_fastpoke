About Us

Our Mission

To empower hiring authorities, top talent, and top specialized recruiters by changing how they connect.

Our Origins

Fastpoke was designed by a group of young professionals and veteran headhunters in Stamford, CT with a major goal: to help connect professionals with smaller, specialized recruitment firms. The platform was also designed to help talented recruiters market themselves to hiring authorities. With months of brainstorming, hard work, and refining, the platform was launched in August of 2020.

What is Fastpoke?

Fastpoke is a platform for professionas to connect with top specialized recruiters. It is specificaly for smaller, specialized (boutique) recruitment firms. If you're a great recruiter or you are looking for one, Fastpoke is the place to be!

We believe the recruiting industry is a valuable industry to any business. With this belief, Fastpoke aims to bring promotion, transparency, and accessibility to great recruiters in the industry. Here, you can browse, message, and refer highly specialized recruiters. If you are seeking career opportunities, career advice, or are looking to hire talent, Fastpoke makes finding top recruiters quick and painless.

Are you a recruiter of a boutique firm? Fastpoke is a platform to increase your exposure and market yourself. Through your recruiter profile, you receive written referrals, showcase your specialties, and track your exposure through analytics.
